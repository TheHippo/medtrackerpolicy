# Privacy Policy for MedTracker

**Last updated:** 17 June 2026

This privacy policy describes how MedTracker ("the app", "we", "our") handles information when you use it. MedTracker is developed by Philipp Klose ("the developer").

## Summary

MedTracker is a single-user medication tracking app designed to work entirely on your device. **The app does not collect, transmit, store remotely, sell, or share any of your personal data with the developer, with any third party, or with any analytics, advertising, or cloud service.** There is no user account, no sign-in, no internet connection required for any feature, and no data ever leaves your device.

## Information We Collect

MedTracker does not collect any information at all. Specifically:

- **No account or registration data.** The app has no sign-in or account system.
- **No analytics.** The app does not use any analytics SDK and does not track how you use the app.
- **No advertising.** The app contains no advertising and no advertising SDKs.
- **No crash or diagnostic reporting services.** The app does not transmit crash logs or diagnostic data to the developer or any third party.
- **No device identifiers, location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive data are accessed or collected.**

## Information You Enter Into the App

To provide its core functionality, MedTracker stores the following information **locally on your device only**, in a private app database that is not accessible to other apps or to the developer:

- Medication names and dosages that you add
- Schedules and reminder times you configure
- A history log of doses marked as taken, snoozed, or missed

This information stays on your device. It is never uploaded to a server, never synced to the cloud, and never shared with the developer or any third party. If you uninstall the app, this data is permanently deleted along with it. The app does not currently offer an export or backup feature, so no copy of this data exists anywhere outside your device.

## Permissions Used

MedTracker requests the following Android permissions, used solely to provide its core reminder functionality on-device:

| Permission | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To show medication reminder notifications at scheduled times. |
| `SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM` | To trigger reminders at the exact time you scheduled them. |
| `RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED` | To re-register your reminders after your device restarts. |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | To briefly wake the device so a reminder can be reliably delivered. |

None of these permissions are used to collect, transmit, or share data. They exist only to deliver on-device, locally-scheduled notifications.

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

MedTracker does not share any data with third parties, because it does not collect or transmit any data in the first place. The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking libraries.

## Children's Privacy

MedTracker does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children. Since the app stores all data locally on-device and never transmits it anywhere, no personal information from any user — including children — is ever collected, processed, or shared by the developer.

## Data Security

Because all data created in MedTracker remains on your device and is never transmitted, the security of that data depends on the security of your own device (e.g. screen lock, device encryption). The developer does not have access to, and cannot retrieve, any data you enter into the app.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All data is retained locally on your device for as long as the app remains installed. You can delete all app data at any time via your device's Android Settings (Settings → Apps → MedTracker → Storage → Clear Data) or by uninstalling the app, which permanently removes all medication, schedule, and history data stored by the app.
